Retrieval services

Retrieval services

  • For emergency advice and paediatric or neonatal transfers

    New South Wales:

    NSW Newborn and paediatric Emergency Transport Service (NETS) 1300 36 25 00

    Queensland:

    View Queensland Paediatric Emergency Care Escalation and advice outside of ED

    or search for “CHQ transport triage”

    South Australia:

    Paediatric and neonatal clinical advice and retrieval is centrally coordinated through MedSTAR Emergency Medical Retrieval

    13STAR (13 78 27) is staffed by consultant paediatricians and neonatologists with access to specialist advice

    Victoria:

    The Paediatric Infant Perinatal Emergency Retrieval (PIPER) Service: 1300 137 650.
